About AATRAL 2K26

AATRAL 2K26 is a prestigious National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Electrogenesis Society, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Thanthai Periyar Government Institute of Technology, Vellore.

Featuring 6 technical and 6 non-technical events, AATRAL 2K26 challenges students to push boundaries — from designing circuits from memory to pitching absurd products, from live technical talks to treasure hunts. One registration, all events. Win cash prizes up to ₹15,000!
